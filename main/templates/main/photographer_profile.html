About

"I believe a wedding photo should feel like a memory, not a pose."

Based in Kathmandu, Sarita has spent eight years photographing weddings across Nepal — from intimate Newari ceremonies to large multi-day celebrations in Pokhara and the Terai. Her style blends candid documentary moments with quiet, editorial portraits, shot mostly in natural light. She works solo or with a second shooter depending on the size of your day.
